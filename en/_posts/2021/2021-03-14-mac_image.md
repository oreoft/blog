## Online installer USB

I‚Äôll share how to make a macOS online installer USB on both Windows and macOS. It‚Äôs basically the same‚Äîonly the flashing tool differs (since the OS is different). ### Win

1. Insert the USB drive.
2. Download [imageUSB](https://cloud.189.cn/t/riaQJf2EzYJj), then open it.
3. Select the image and the USB drive.

![image-20200929115431485](https://mypicgogo.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/tuchuang/20200929115431.png)

### Mac

2. Download [balenaEtcher](https://cloud.189.cn/t/AB3Ybufumeqy), then open it.
3. Select the 10.15 boot image, and choose your target drive.

   ![image-20210410183926989](https://mypicgogo.oss-cn-hangzhou.aliyuncs.com/tuchuang20210410183927.png)

## Full offline image USB

Xiaobing‚Äôs full image USB has two visible partitions and one hidden partition:

- The first visible partition is the macOS installer image volume‚Äîall installation files are inside.
- The second visible partition is a PE boot volume. Xiaobing thoughtfully includes PE‚Äîyou can even use it to install Windows.
- The third hidden partition is the EFI partition. You need to mount it and then put your EFI in there.

Whether you‚Äôre making it on Windows or macOS, the main thing is to download the image you like from [Xiaobing Image Repository](https://download.shaoxingshare.com/t/l0mvW0M1gx).
